FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you might want to know before you order. Still curious? Send us a note.

How do I start a custom order?

Send us your idea through the custom order form — the item you want, your design, logo, or monogram, the colors, and how many you need. We'll reply with a quote and a digitized proof for you to approve before anything is stitched.

What is your turnaround time?

Most custom orders ship within 1–2 weeks after you approve your proof, depending on the size of the order and the time of year. Large or bulk orders may take longer; rush options may be available — just ask.

Can I supply my own garments or blanks?

Yes. You're welcome to bring your own items to embroider, or we can source quality blanks for you. Customer-supplied items are stitched at your own risk, since we can't replace an item we didn't provide if something goes wrong during stitching.

What artwork or files do you need?

A clear, high-resolution image of your design or logo works to start — a vector file (AI, EPS, SVG, or PDF) is ideal. New designs are digitized for embroidery, which may include a one-time digitizing fee. We'll confirm everything on your proof before stitching.

Do you do bulk, team, or event orders?

Absolutely. Staff uniforms, team apparel, bridal parties, family events, and company gifts are some of our favorite projects. Bulk orders get per-piece pricing and a single point of contact from proof to delivery.

How do I care for embroidered items?

Turn the item inside out, wash cold on a gentle cycle, and tumble dry low or lay flat. Avoid ironing directly over the embroidery. Care instructions are included with every order.

What is your return policy on personalized items?

Because custom and personalized pieces are made just for you, they can't be returned or refunded unless they arrive defective or we made an error. If anything is wrong with your order, contact us right away and we'll make it right. See our Terms for details.

Do you ship, and where are you located?

Yes, we ship your finished pieces carefully packed and pressed. Local pickup may be available — reach out and we'll let you know what's possible.
