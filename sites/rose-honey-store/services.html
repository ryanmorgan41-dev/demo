Services & Pricing

Custom embroidery, start to finish.

Bring an idea, a logo, a monogram, or a milestone — we'll handle the design, digitizing, and stitching, and send you something you'll be proud to give or wear.

What's Included

Every order comes with care.

  • A digitized proof for your approval before we stitch
  • Thread-color matching to your brand or palette
  • Placement and sizing guidance
  • Quality blanks — yours or sourced for you
  • A final quality check and careful press
  • Care instructions packed with every piece

What We Embroider

Pick your canvas.

Apparel

Personalized apparel

Sweatshirts, tees, polos, jackets, hats, and aprons embroidered with names, logos, monograms, dates, or custom artwork. Great for gifts, small businesses, and matching sets.

Monograms & Home

Monograms & home goods

Towels, robes, napkins, throws, and linens finished with classic or modern monograms — wedding gifts, housewarmings, and the quiet luxuries of a well-dressed home.

Baby & Gifts

Baby & keepsakes

Name onesies, bibs, burp cloths, baby blankets, and milestone gifts — soft, washable, and made to be saved long after they're outgrown.

The Process

How a custom order works.

Share your idea

Tell us the item, the design or monogram, colors, and how many you need.

Quote & proof

We send a price and a digitized preview to approve before anything is stitched.

Stitch & check

We embroider your pieces and quality-check every one by hand.

Pack & ship

Pressed, wrapped, and sent to your door with care instructions.
