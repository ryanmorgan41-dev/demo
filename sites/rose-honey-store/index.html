Why Rose & Honey

Thoughtful pieces, made to be kept.

Anyone can press a logo onto a shirt. We dwell on the details — thread color, placement, the weight of the stitch — so what you receive feels personal, looks polished, and holds up wash after wash. Whether it's one heirloom gift or a run for your whole team, every piece gets the same hand.

How It Works

From your idea to a finished piece.

1

Share your idea

Send your concept, monogram, logo, or photo and tell us the item, colors, and quantity.

2

Approve your proof

We digitize the design and send a preview with thread colors and placement for your sign-off.

3

We stitch it

Your design is embroidered onto quality blanks — yours or sourced for you — and quality-checked.

4

It ships to you

Carefully pressed, packed, and on its way, with care instructions tucked inside.
