Have something worth passing on?

Consigning, made simple

Downsizing, redecorating, or just ready to move a quality piece along? Bring it to Consignology and let it find its next home without the hassle of listing, messaging strangers, and hauling it to a market yourself.

Bring photos, or the piece itself, to the shop in the Union Block. We’ll tell you honestly whether it’s a fit.

  1. First

    Bring it by

    Stop in with the piece or a few photos. We look it over and let you know right away if it belongs on the floor.

  2. Then

    We price & display

    We set a fair price, clean it up, and give it a spot on the showroom floor where the right buyer will actually see it.

  3. Payday

    You get your share

    When it sells, your cut is yours. No separate listing to manage, no strangers at your door.

A downtown fixture

Twelve years in the historic Union Block.

Consignology has kept a corner of downtown Brigham City interesting for over a decade — a place where neighbors bring in furniture and home goods they love but no longer have room for, and other neighbors carry them back out the door. The building has watched Main Street change; the treasure hunt inside hasn’t.

It’s a shop built on trust, fair prices, and treating consignors and shoppers the same way you’d want to be treated. The reviews say it plainly.
