Precision restoration

Damage doesn't leave. It gets corrected.

Every job runs the same disciplined path: read the damage, match the color to the panel, and rebuild the finish until the repair disappears into the factory line. One standard, every car.

  1. Assess

    Read the damage

    We walk the vehicle with you, document every affected panel, and quote it straight. Free estimate, no pressure, and the insurance claim started for you.

  2. Color match

    Match the panel

    Color is mixed and tested against your actual paint, then blended so the new finish reads as one continuous surface with the factory coat around it.

  3. Refinish

    Rebuild the finish

    Body, clear coat and detail work brought back to spec, then cleaned and handed over so you would never know the panel had been touched.
